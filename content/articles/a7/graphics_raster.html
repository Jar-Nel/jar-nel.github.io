Raster graphics formats define a grid of pixels at a set resolution. Resizing these images can cause a loss of image information that can result in a loss of detail or a blurry image.

Raster graphics aren't computationally intensive and are a popular choice for computer imagery and video game textures. They are widely used despite their limitations.

Popular raster image formats include: (click on the formats to learn more about them)

  • GIF (Graphics Interchange Format)
  • JPEG (Joint Photographic Experts Group)
  • PNG (Portable Network Graphics)
  • WebP
