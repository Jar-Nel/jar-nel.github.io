Primer for Paint

Paint.NET is a free photo editing software for Windows. While it has some limitations compared to it's commercial bretheren, Paint.NET is just about perfect for the small manipulations a web designer might need to make to an image.

Paint.NET supports layers, unlimited undo, special effects, text composition, and supports a wide vareity of image formats and compression.

Paint.NET also performs very well on lower end hardware. Every image on this site has gone through some kind of processing in Paint.NET.

If you're interested in learning more about Paint.NET, or downloading a copy for yourself, you can get it at the official website https://www.getpaint.net
